PROFESSIONAL SERVICE

CLEARPATH Outdoor was founded by a Purdue Landscape Architect with 25 years of outdoor project experience. We're stump removal specialists and working to create a new standard of service in stump removal.

OWNER OPERATED

We're an owner operated business. You'll work directly with the owner throughout your project. Have a question? Please ask!

RESPONSIVE

We'll reply to your request today and most projects are scheduled within a week. With a powerful machine, we'll work quickly to minimize the noise at your home.

PRO EQUIPMENT

We have the latest professional stump grinding equipment. Our compact machine will allow us to reach stumps with little to no lawn damage and we can easily fit through a 36" gate.

SAFE AND INSURED

We follow pro safety standards, including utility location, personal safety gear and guard screens around our machine to protect your home. We're also fully insured to work at your home or business.

RECYCLE!

We'll grind your stump and surface roots to a depth of 8"-12", leaving you with a neat pile of wood chips in it's place. The valuable mulch can be later used in a natural bed area or compost pile. Recycle your tree stump!
